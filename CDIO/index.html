Greeting from DNUI

Welcome to 2019 CDIO Asian regional meeting website! It is a great honor for Dalian Neusoft University of Information (DNUI) to host this meeting. DNUI is a young and vibrant application-oriented university dedicated to IT engineering education, located in Dalian city, an international, beautiful coastal city in Northeast China. Ever since we introduced CDIO and initiated TOPCARES-CDIO education reform in 2008, it has been a decade. We hope that, taking this opportunity of 2019 Asian regional meeting, we will share experiences and explore possibilities of future CDIO developments with fellow collaborators, engineering education performers and industry practitioners; that this meeting will provide a platform for engineering education institutions in Asia to communicate and cooperate; that every attendee will have refreshing and fruitful experience during their stay in Dalian.

In this site, you will find information about the meeting, including agenda, venues, program, organizations, registration, contribution, and important notices. You can also get to know DNUI through this site. Please stay tuned for upcoming progress!
