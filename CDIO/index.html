Welcome to 2019 CDIO Asian Regional Meeting

Dalian NeusoftUniversity of Information (DNUI) is a private university founded by NeusoftHoldings with the Ministry
of Education’s approval. DNUI offers tertiaryeducation programs across a number of disciplines including
management, digitalarts and liberal arts with a strong focus on engineering.
