About DNUI

Dalian Neusoft University of Information (DNUI) is a private university founded in 2000 by Neusoft Holdings with the Ministry of Education's approval. The university is located at Dalian, a beautiful coastal city and economic center in Northeast China. DNUI has fifteen academic departments and schools offering undergraduate degrees, international education programs and postgraduate programs across a number of disciplines including management, digital arts and liberal arts with a strong focus on IT engineering. Over 14,000 students are currently enrolled at DNUI. The average annual employment rate of the last three years is over 93%.
